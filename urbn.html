ABOUT URBN

URBN is a dynamic café by day and a vibrant bar by night, providing a one-of-a-kind experience for all patrons. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be enveloped in a warm, inviting atmosphere that is perfect for savoring a fresh cup of coffee or enjoying a hand-crafted cocktail. During the day, we specialize in serving expertly brewed coffee and a selection of fresh pastries, providing a perfect boost to start your day. As the sun sets, our bar comes alive, featuring an extensive selection of alcoholic drinks, including signature cocktails and a curated selection of wines and spirits. URBN is the perfect place to relax, unwind, and enjoy delicious drinks and great company.
