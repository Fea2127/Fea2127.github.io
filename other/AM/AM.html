ABOUT URBN
URBN is a dynamic cafe by day and a vibrant bar by night, providing a one-of-a-kind experience for all patrons. From the moment you step through our doors, you will be greeted by a warm and inviting atmosphere that is perfect for savoring a fresh cup of coffee or enjoying a hand-crafted cocktail. During the day, we specialize in serving expertly crafted coffee beverages made from premium beans, providing a perfect boost to start your day. As the sun sets, our bar comes alive, featuring an extensive selection of alcoholic drinks, including signature cocktails and fine wines. With its eclectic and comfortable ambiance, UNHINGED is the perfect place to relax, unwind, and enjoy delicious drinks and great company.
