Charlie G. Calupig – proud son of a farmer from Barangay Layasin, Cavinti, Laguna. His winning streak began with Ginoong Cavinti 2024, then captured the provincial crown as Ginoong Laguna 2025. In March 2026, he represented Cavinti at Lakan ng Laguna. Beyond titles, Charlie is a print model, tourism advocate, and volleyball athlete studying at PATTS College of Aeronautics. He organizes youth sports events in his barangay and appeared on GMA's Family Feud Philippines. He credits his father’s hard work as his greatest inspiration.
